I'm an Assistant Professor at School of Information Engineering of Xi'an University since 2023. I received my Ph.D. degree at the computer science and technology of Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), under the supervision of Prof. Jinsong Han and Prof. Wei Xi. I received my master degree at school of electronics and control engineering of Chang'an University (CHD), under the supervision of Prof. Jian Xiao. I received my bachelor degree at school of automation of Xi'an University of posts and telecommunications (XUPT).

My research interests include RFID sensing, visible light communication and mobile computing.
